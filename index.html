Cats in ancient Egypt were represented in social and religious practices of Ancient Egypt for more than 30 centuries. Several Ancient Egyptian deities were depcted and sculptred with cat like heads such as Mafdet, Bastet and Sekhmet, representing justice, fertility and power.
